Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026 Last updated: April 26, 2026  |  App version: 1.0

NatureScan ("we", "our" or "the app") is a mobile application that helps you understand what is in your food and cosmetics by scanning ingredient lists and providing AI-powered health scores. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By downloading or using NatureScan, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

NatureScan does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number or any other personal identifying information.

Camera and photos

When you scan an ingredient list, the app uses your device camera to capture an image or extract text. This data is processed as follows:

  • On-device OCR (text recognition) — handled entirely on your device using Google ML Kit. The image never leaves your phone for this step.
  • AI analysis — the extracted ingredient text (or the image itself as a fallback) is sent to the Anthropic API to generate a health score. See Section 3 for details.
  • Photos are not stored — NatureScan does not save photos to your gallery or our servers. Images are used only for the current scan and discarded immediately after.

Scan history and saved lists

Your scan results, My Healthy Choices and Products to Avoid lists are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Usage data

We do not use analytics tools or track how you use the app. We do not collect crash reports, session data, or any behavioural information.

2. How We Use Your Information

The only data processing that occurs in NatureScan is:

  • Sending ingredient text or images to the Anthropic API to generate a naturalness or safety score for the product you are scanning.
  • Storing your scan history and saved product lists locally on your device so you can refer back to them.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, marketing or any purpose other than operating the core features of the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Anthropic API

NatureScan uses the Anthropic Claude API to analyse ingredient lists. When you scan a product, the ingredient text extracted from the label is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing.

  • Data sent: ingredient text from product labels
  • Data NOT sent: your identity, location, device ID or any personal information
  • Purpose: generating health scores for the scanned product
  • Anthropic's privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy
Important: Ingredient lists are product information, not personal information. We never send anything that could identify you to Anthropic or any other third party.

Google ML Kit

NatureScan uses Google ML Kit Text Recognition for on-device OCR. This runs entirely on your device and does not send images or text to Google.

  • Google ML Kit privacy information: developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms

No other third-party services

NatureScan does not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, advertising networks, crash reporting tools or any other third-party data collection service.

4. Data Storage and Security

All your personal data — scan history, saved products, preferences — is stored locally on your Android device. It is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers or databases.

When you uninstall NatureScan, all locally stored data is permanently deleted from your device.

The only external transmission that occurs is sending ingredient text to the Anthropic API, which is done over an encrypted HTTPS connection.

5. Children's Privacy

NatureScan is rated for everyone (3+) on the Google Play Store. The app does not collect any personal information from users of any age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Permissions We Request

NatureScan requests the following Android permissions:

  • CAMERA — to photograph ingredient lists for scanning. Required for the core scanning feature.
  • INTERNET — to send ingredient text to the Anthropic API and receive health scores.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — to save share card images to your device when you choose to share a scan result. Only used when you actively tap the Share button.

We do not request access to your contacts, location, microphone, calendar, messages or any other sensitive data.

7. Your Rights

Because NatureScan stores all data locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Access — all your data is visible within the app in the My Health section.
  • Delete individual scans — swipe left on any scan in My Health to delete it.
  • Delete all data — tap the trash icon in My Health to clear your entire history.
  • Delete everything — uninstalling NatureScan permanently removes all app data from your device.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade or share your data with any third party for commercial purposes. The only data transfer that occurs is sending ingredient text to the Anthropic API solely to power the scoring feature.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy — for example when we introduce premium features or new functionality — we will update the effective date at the top of this page and notify users via an in-app notice.

Continued use of NatureScan after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Disclaimer

NatureScan provides ingredient scores as a helpful guide based on AI analysis. Scores are not a substitute for professional medical, nutritional or dermatological advice. Always consult a qualified professional for health decisions.
